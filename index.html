Welcome!

I am currently a graduate student at Oregon State University pursuing a PhD in Computer Science with a focus on machine learning. I just graduated top of my class from Gonzaga University with a BS in Computer Engineering. I love programming, just as much as I love skiing and fly fishing in the Northwest.

Right now, I am very interested in how machine learning can be used to analyze data to help people. I just joined the Information Processing Group under the direction of Dr. V John Mathews at OSU. We are researching machine learning algorithms that enable amputees and quadriplegics to control robotic hands.
